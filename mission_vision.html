Mission and Vision Statement

Our Mission

The Brahmanbaria Community of North America, Inc. (BCNA) dedicates to the well-being of all Non Resident Bangladeshi resides in USA and cares and integrates systems of social development services that facilitates human skill development and improves the quality of life that efforts contributing to a world which recognizes, respects, and values the socioeconomic, political, legal and environmental aspirations of marginalized and vulnerable communities on an equality basis that promotes a sustainable, equitable prosperous and peaceful society.

Our Vision

The Brahmanbaria Community of North America, Inc. (BCNA) endeavors to empower and improve the quality of life of the marginalized and vulnerable communities by capacity building, promotion of best practices and partnership building, advocacy, and by launching sustainable and replicable innovative projects.
